Work Preferences and Options for Collaboration

When it comes to completing tasks, I offer two approaches to suit your preferences and desired level of involvement:

1. Remote Live Sessions

During a remote live session, we can securely connect either my computer to yours or vice versa to share desktop controls. This setup allows you to oversee my work in real time, observe the steps I take, and even learn repeatable processes if you'd like. It also ensures enhanced security, as I can access sensitive information directly on your system without needing to store it elsewhere.

Alternatively, connecting to my computer gives you the flexibility to interact with the tools I use, should you choose to do so. In either case, we can work collaboratively, with all time spent fully transparent and verifiable.

2. Screen Recording

If you prefer to delegate tasks without live involvement, I can record the entire session. This way, you have a video record of the work completed, enabling you to review and learn the steps I performed if needed.
